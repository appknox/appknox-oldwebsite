About Us

We're a community of ethical hackers dedicated to unmatched mobile app security.

Our Story

The idea "AppKnox"

When?

Majority of the apps in the app stores
were vulnerable to security issues

Who?

Hackers were exploiting the open
source Android OS to find vulnerabilities
and inject malware, security issues
in iOS apps

Why?

AppKnox born with the idea of building a
tool to help app owners secure their apps

What?

Found over 1,000 threats and audited over 900
applications, including the ones developed by Facebook,
Google, Microsoft, Skype, Yahoo and PayPal

Our Mission

To outthink and outsmart unethical hackers that exploit different cyber channels, and to provide a safe operating environment for businesses around the globe. Appknox aims at building a safe and secure mobile ecosystem for everyone.

Our People and Culture

Appknox is completely dedicated to mobile app security. We share this common vision throughout the Appknox team. We let our culture drive positive results throughout the organization and bring out the best within each one of us. We have a team of young, experienced, enthusiastic and intelligent anti-hacking professionals that will do anything it takes to keep our clients secure.

We embrace diversity and reject discrimination and inequality of any kind. We are passionate and strive to reflect the quality of our work with the product and services we render.
